Help Centre

Frequently Asked
Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering, delivery, concierge gifting, and more. Can't find your answer? Talk to us.

Ordering
How do I place an order?
Browse our shop, add items to your cart, and check out securely. For concierge or corporate orders, please use our enquiry form instead.
Can I personalise my gift?
Yes — most of our products support personalised messages. Add your note at checkout and we'll include it beautifully printed on a Barefoot Gifting card.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept bank transfers, card payments (Visa & Mastercard), and Paystack. All transactions are secure and encrypted.
Can I modify or cancel my order?
Orders can be modified or cancelled within 2 hours of placement. After that, your order may already be in production. Contact us immediately on WhatsApp for urgent changes.
Delivery
Do you deliver outside Abuja?
Yes! We deliver nationwide across Nigeria. Standard delivery takes 2–5 business days depending on your location. Abuja orders qualify for free delivery on orders over ₦25,000.
How long does delivery take?
Abuja: 1–2 business days. Lagos, Port Harcourt, Kano: 2–4 business days. Other locations: 3–6 business days. Express same-day delivery is available in Abuja for an additional fee.
How is my gift packaged?
Every order is wrapped in our branded tissue paper, sealed with a Barefoot Gifting sticker, and placed in our signature gift box — ready to give, straight from our hands to yours.
Concierge Gifting
What is Concierge Gifting?
Our Concierge service is a fully personalised gifting experience — you tell us about the recipient, the occasion, and your budget, and we curate, assemble, and deliver a bespoke gift box on your behalf.
How much does Concierge Gifting cost?
Concierge boxes start from ₦25,000. The final price depends on your chosen items, curation complexity, and delivery destination. We'll always confirm pricing before we begin.
How far in advance should I book?
We recommend at least 3–5 business days for concierge orders. For peak seasons (Christmas, Valentine's Day, Mother's Day), book 2–3 weeks in advance to guarantee your slot.
Returns & Refunds
What is your returns policy?
We accept returns on unopened, undamaged items within 7 days of delivery. Personalised items, greeting cards, and perishable goods are non-returnable. Contact us to initiate a return.
What if my order arrives damaged?
We're so sorry — that's never okay. Send us photos of the damage within 48 hours of delivery via WhatsApp or email and we'll arrange a replacement or full refund immediately.
Do you offer gift receipts?
Yes. You can request a gift receipt at checkout — it shows the items without prices, so the recipient can exchange if needed without awkwardness.
