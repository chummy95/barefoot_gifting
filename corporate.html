How to Place an Order

From brief to bow

A simple five-step process that takes your order from idea to delivered — with no stress on your end.

01
Submit an Inquiry

Fill out our inquiry form with your name and organization, type of order, quantity, event date or deadline, budget (optional), branding requirements, and preferred communication method.

02
Consultation & Quotation

Our team reviews your request and prepares a customised proposal — product recommendations, design suggestions, quantity options, timelines, and pricing — for your review and approval.

03
Design & Approval

Once your deposit is received, we begin the design process. You'll receive digital mockups, design proofs, and material recommendations. Production begins only after final approval.

04
Production

Your order moves into production. Timelines vary by product type, quantity, customisation requirements, and material availability — we'll keep you informed throughout.

05
Delivery or Pickup

Once completed, your order is delivered to your preferred location or made available for pickup. We'll confirm every detail with you before dispatch.
