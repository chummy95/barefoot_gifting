Our Story

From Abuja, with love
and a lot of ribbon.

Barefoot Gifting was founded in 2022 with a single, deeply felt belief: that giving a gift should feel as meaningful as receiving one. We saw a gap in the Nigerian gifting landscape — beautiful, thoughtful, culturally resonant gifts that told the recipient they were truly seen.

What started as a small passion project grew into something far greater than we imagined. Today, Barefoot Gifting serves hundreds of givers across Nigeria — from intimate birthday moments to large corporate gifting programs — all anchored in the same spirit of intention.

We draw deeply from Nigerian culture, from the warmth of celebration embedded in our traditions, from the joy of naming ceremonies and graduations and weddings. Every box we send is a love letter to those moments — and to the people who make them matter.

Our Founder

The woman
behind the bow.

I started Barefoot Gifting because I've always believed that the things we give each other should mean something. For as long as I can remember, my mother has written in every single card she's ever given me — the day I was born, the time, and my weight — even now that I'm very much in my 30s. And so it makes sense that I've been drawn to stories, memories, celebrations, and the little details that make people feel seen.

What began as a love for creating thoughtful cards and meaningful gifts slowly grew into something bigger. I noticed that many gifts and event souvenirs were beautiful in the moment but quickly forgotten. I wanted to create something different. Something that sparked emotion. Something that made people pause, smile, remember, and hold on a little longer. That desire became Barefoot Gifting.

"A gift is more than an exchange of items. It is a way of saying, I thought about you."

The name "Barefoot" reflects the heart of our brand. There is something honest about being barefoot — it speaks of home, authenticity, childhood memories, and the moments when we are most ourselves. We want every product we make to carry that same warmth and familiarity.

Our mission: To create and curate goods that evoke emotion, celebrate connection, and become cherished keepsakes for generations to come.

Bella Fayomi
Founder & Heritage Designer, Barefoot Gifting
What We Stand For

Our values

Four principles guide every gift we curate, every box we build, and every relationship we nurture.

Intention Over Occasion

We believe the best gifts don't need a special occasion — they need a special thought. Every product we choose and every box we build starts with the question: does this feel meaningful?

Nigerian Identity

We're proudly Abuja-born, Nigeria-rooted. Our gifts reflect the richness of our culture — the warmth, the celebrations, the colour, the pride of giving something homegrown and world-class at once.

Premium Experience

From the quality of our products to the way they're wrapped, every touchpoint is considered. Because the experience of receiving a Barefoot gift should be as beautiful as opening one.

Community of Givers

We're building more than a gifting brand. We're nurturing a community of people who understand that giving is an act of love — and who want to do it beautifully, thoughtfully, and with purpose.
