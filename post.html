The Ultimate Mother's Day Gift Guide for 2025 — from Abuja with Love

✍️ Barefoot Gifting Team 📅 May 2025 ⏱ 8 min read

Mothers in Nigeria don't just raise children — they hold families together, hold communities together, hold us together when we can't hold ourselves. This Mother's Day, we want to help you say thank you in a way that actually lands.

We've put together this guide for everyone from the last-minute gifter (no judgment) to the person who planned ahead and still isn't sure they've chosen right. Spoiler: you probably have.

Why the Gift Matters More Than You Think

There's a version of gifting that's just obligation — you buy something, she says thank you, everyone moves on. Then there's intentional gifting: you choose something that says "I see you, not just as my mother, but as a person." That second kind changes things.

"The best gifts don't just say I love you. They say I was paying attention."

— Barefoot Gifting

Our Little Luxes boxes — curated, wrapped, and ready to deliver.

Our Top Picks for Mum This Year

1. The For Your Heart Box — ₦75,000

This one is for the sentimental mum — the one who still has every birthday card you've ever given her. The For Your Heart box includes a personalised keychain, A5 notepad, fridge magnet, scented candle, and a handwritten card. It arrives wrapped in our signature paper and ribbon, ready to make her cry in the best way.

"I ordered this for my mum and she called me three times to say thank you. She loves the keychain most."

2. Graced and Grounded — ₦80,000

For the mum who does everything quietly, without fanfare. A mini mug, journal, scented candle, handwritten note, and a treat jar. It's grounding, gentle, and says you don't have to keep it all together alone.

3. Some "Thank You Love" — ₦85,000

This one goes out to the mother who has done too much for too long without enough acknowledgement. Scented candle, an engraved chopping board, fine teas, and a handwritten note. It says: I see what you do, and I'm grateful.
