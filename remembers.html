Questions

A calmer way to stay ahead of every milestone.

If you are already gifting through Barefoot, this becomes the memory layer behind it. If you are new here, it is an easy place to start.

Can I save fewer than 10 dates?

Yes. You can save as few as one date. The 10-date limit is just the maximum allowed under one email registration.

What if I need to change a date later?

Use the same email address and submit the form again. We will update your saved reminder profile with the latest list you send in.

Will Barefoot also help me choose the gift?

Yes. That is part of the idea. When we remind you, we can also guide you toward the right gift, card, or concierge option for the moment.
